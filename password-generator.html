Why use a Password Generator?

Using the same password for multiple websites is one of the most common security mistakes. If one website is hacked, all your other accounts become vulnerable. A strong, unique password for each account is the best protection.

A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and contain a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and symbols. Our generator creates truly random passwords that are extremely difficult to guess or crack.

Your passwords are generated entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They are never sent to our servers, stored in any database, or tracked in any way.
