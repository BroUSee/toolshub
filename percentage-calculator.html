How to calculate percentage?

To find X% of Y: multiply Y by X and divide by 100. For example, 25% of 400 = (400 × 25) / 100 = 100.

To find what percentage A is of B: divide A by B and multiply by 100. For example, 50 is what % of 200? = (50/200) × 100 = 25%.
