EMI Calculator

Calculate your monthly EMI for any loan — home, car, personal or education. Get full breakdown of total interest and total payment.

Monthly EMI

EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1) where P is principal loan amount, r is monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100), and n is number of monthly installments.

For example: ₹5,00,000 loan at 8.5% for 5 years (60 months) = EMI of approximately ₹10,224 per month.
