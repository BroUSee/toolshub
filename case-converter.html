When to use each case?

UPPERCASE is used for headings, abbreviations and emphasis. lowercase is used for casual writing and URLs. Title Case capitalizes first letter of each word — used for titles and headings. Sentence case only capitalizes first word — used for normal sentences and paragraphs.
